About Fluid

Fluid is a technical blog exploring the intersection of technology, engineering, philosophy, and creative thinking.

What You'll Find Here

  • Software Engineering: Deep dives into programming concepts, architectural patterns, and engineering best practices
  • Web Development: Explorations in modern web technologies, frameworks, and development workflows
  • Technology Trends: Analysis of emerging technologies, tools, and their impact on how we build software
  • Startup Insights: Reflections on building products, startups, and the tech ecosystem
  • Philosophy: Explorations of ideas that shape how we think about technology and its role in society
  • Books & Learning: Notes and reflections from technical books, papers, and continuous learning

About This Blog

Fluid is built with Jekyll, hosted on GitHub Pages, and designed for readability and performance. The name "Fluid" reflects the flowing nature of ideas and thoughts as they move from concept to code, from theory to practice, from question to insight.

All content is written by hand, with care and attention to clarity. If you find something useful, feel free to share it. If you have thoughts or questions, reach out via email.
