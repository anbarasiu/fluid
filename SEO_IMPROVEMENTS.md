## Jekyll SEO Best Practices

1. **Post Front Matter**: Always include title, description, and tags
2. **Images**: Use descriptive alt text
3. **Internal Linking**: Link between related posts
4. **Headings**: Use proper hierarchy (H2, H3)
5. **Descriptions**: Keep under 160 characters
6. **Tags**: Use relevant, consistent tags
